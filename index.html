American Sign Language (ASL) translation system developed during the Samsung Innovation Campus x Misk Foundation AI Program! Yad-tech uses a two-part approach to bring ASL into real-time text. This combination allowed us to create a smooth, responsive experience where static signs are accurately interpreted, and dynamic gestures are handled in real-time.
